Common Symptoms to Look For:

  • Nitrogen: Yellowing of older (lower) leaves, pale green color
  • Phosphorus: Purple or dark green leaves, especially in cool weather
  • Potassium: Yellowing and browning at leaf edges and tips
  • Magnesium: Yellowing between leaf veins (older leaves)
  • Iron: Yellowing between leaf veins (young leaves), bleached appearance
  • Calcium: Death of growing points, blossom end rot on fruits
